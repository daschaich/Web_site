Research — Topological phases and topological phase transitions

Image: Signatures of a phase transition in a simple field theory that involves four-fermion interactions, from arXiv:1609.08541. Unlike conventional systems, the symmetries of the system are the same in both phases on either side of the transition. Despite the identical symmetries, the fermions are massive for large four-fermion coupling G but massless for small G.
