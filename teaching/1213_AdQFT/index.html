Advanced Quantum Field Theory, 2012—2013

Fridays 10:30 in G1B31

Topics

  • Basic group theory, non-abelian gauge theory
  • Standard model
  • Anomalies, chiral gauge theories, Schwinger model
  • Solitons and instantons
  • Group theory applications to GUTs and Little Higgs
  • Supersymmetry
  • Large N
  • Seiberg duality
  • Conformal field theory
  • AdS/CFT
